How do I book tickets online?

Simply navigate to our Book page, enter your route and travel date, select your preferred seat(s) from the interactive map, fill in passenger details, and confirm to generate your digital ticket instantly.

What is the luggage allowance policy?

Passengers are allowed to bring up to 20kg of check-in luggage stored in the bus cargo compartment and 1 small hand-carry bag inside the cabin for free. Additional luggage fees may apply for overweight items.

Are the buses equipped with Wifi and power outlets?

Yes, all our Luxury A/C Coaches and Premium Rosa Minibuses are equipped with free high-speed passenger Wi-Fi and individual USB charging outlets at each seat.

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 12 hours before the scheduled departure time through our customer support hotline or by logging into your passenger portal.
